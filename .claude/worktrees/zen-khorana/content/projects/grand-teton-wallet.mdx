A Figma concept for a bifold wallet inspired by my 2024 trip to Grand Teton National Park. I traced the mountain contour from a photograph and turned it into a leather deboss pattern. The constraints were tight: two leather tones, one stitch color, 6–8 card slots. I wanted it to feel like something you'd actually carry, not just a design exercise.

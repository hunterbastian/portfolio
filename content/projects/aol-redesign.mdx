# AOL Redesign

A modern mobile email app concept that reimagines the classic AOL email experience for today's users.

## Challenge

Redesign AOL's email interface for mobile, maintaining brand recognition while delivering a clean, contemporary user experience that competes with modern email apps.

## Design Features

- **Clean inbox layout**: Prioritized email list with clear visual hierarchy
- **Tab-based navigation**: Primary and Search tabs for easy email management
- **Modern typography**: Readable fonts optimized for mobile screens
- **Minimalist UI**: Reduced clutter while maintaining essential functionality
- **Action buttons**: Star favorites and compose new emails with intuitive controls

## Solution

Created a mobile-first email interface that honors AOL's legacy while providing a streamlined, intuitive experience. The design features a light color scheme, organized inbox view, and familiar email management patterns that modern users expect.

## Results

A successful modernization that bridges AOL's nostalgic brand with contemporary mobile design standards, demonstrating how classic digital products can evolve while maintaining their identity.

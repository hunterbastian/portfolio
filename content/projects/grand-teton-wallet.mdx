# Grand Teton Wallet

A minimalist wallet design featuring mountain silhouettes inspired by the Grand Teton range.

## Design

The wallet features a clean, nature-inspired aesthetic with a distinctive mountain silhouette that captures the essence of the American West. The design includes:

- **Unfolded view**: Organized compartments for cash, ID, and cards
- **Main card slot**: Easy-access pocket for frequently used cards
- **Material**: Premium leather-like finish in warm earth tones
- **Front design**: Minimalist mountain range silhouette

## Features

- Multiple card slots for organization
- Dedicated ID window
- Cash compartment
- Slim profile for everyday carry

## Inspiration

Drawing from the majestic peaks of Grand Teton National Park, this wallet embodies simplicity and natural beauty in a functional everyday object.

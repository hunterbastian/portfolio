## Overview

Aeth Lands is a small interactive web piece focused on stylized floating environments. The project plays with low-poly forms, soft lighting, and a simple world-switching interface to make each biome feel like a tiny collectible place.

## Direction

I wanted this one to feel light and toy-like rather than realistic. The appeal comes from scale, shape, and atmosphere more than complexity, so the scene stays intentionally simple and readable.

## What It Explores

This project leans into playful 3D composition, quick interaction loops, and the idea that a single environment can feel memorable even when it's reduced to just a few forms and materials.
